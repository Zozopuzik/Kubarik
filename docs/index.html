About

A cozy block-puzzle game for one-handed play.

Drop pieces onto a 7×7 grid. Snap blocks into rows and columns. Chain combos until the board explodes with particles, screen shake, and a praise flash that escalates from NICE to ON FIRE.

Built from scratch in SwiftUI to learn Swift — solo, in a single weekend.

What's inside

39 piece shapes

Lines, squares, L-shapes, big rectangles. Spawn pool widens as your score climbs.

Magnetic snap

Missed by a hair? The piece snaps to the nearest legal cell so the flow never breaks.

Combo chains

100 · 300 · 500 base score for 1 · 2 · 3 lines, plus an escalating bonus for every step.

Dopamine effects

Settle-pop bounce, particle bursts, +N score popup, screen shake, three haptic intensities.

Top-100 leaderboard

Sign in with email to track best scores, total games, lines cleared. Compete or play as a guest.

Privacy first

No analytics, no ads, no third-party tracking. Delete your account from inside the app anytime.
